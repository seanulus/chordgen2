Chordgen 2.0

Fretboard Chord/Scale Generator

Chordgen 2.0 was developed for musicians by musicians.

Instructions: Pick a key and scale and hit submit. Chordgen will generate a virtual fretboard containing all scale tones of that key. It will also generate a list of chords with extensions pertaining to each scale degree.
